About Me

I'm a Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst with over a year of hands-on experience in security monitoring, threat detection, incident investigation, and Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI). At DALAS Tech, I monitor security events, investigate alerts, analyze indicators of compromise (IOCs), and identify attacker behavior through network traffic analysis and OSINT investigations. I also produce technical security reports and map adversary tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) using the MITRE ATT&CK framework.

My expertise includes SIEM monitoring, log analysis, threat hunting, malware analysis, network traffic investigation, and incident response. I'm experienced with security tools such as Wazuh, Wireshark, Zeek, Suricata, MISP, TheHive, Cortex, and VirusTotal to strengthen detection and response capabilities.

Alongside cybersecurity, I'm expanding my software development skills by building modern full-stack applications using the MERN Stack. I hold a BSc in Computer Science from Jimma University and continuously improve my skills through hands-on labs, CTF challenges, and real-world security research.
